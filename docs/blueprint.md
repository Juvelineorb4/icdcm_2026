# **App Name**: ICDCM Digital

## Core Features:

- Informational Landing Page: Showcase the church's identity, mission, vision, and values through a visually appealing landing page.
- Sedes (Locations) & Schedules Display: Display the church's locations and service schedules.
- Ministries Grid: Showcase various ministries within the church.
- Online Connection Section: Provide details and links to online services and devotional content.
- Pastoral Leadership Section: Introduce the pastoral team.
- Prayer Request Submission: Allow visitors to submit prayer requests through a form.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Blue (#0067ac) to convey trust and serenity.
- Accent color: Gold (#f8c300) to transmit light, divinity, and energy; for calls to action, icons, and highlights.
- Section backgrounds: White (#ffffff) as the primary background and cream (#fdec9a) for testimonials or bible verses.
- Headline font: 'Montserrat', a clean sans-serif font.
- Body Font: 'Merriweather', a modern serif for longer texts and verses.
- Sticky header/navigation menu.
- Clean, simple icons in blue (#0067ac) to represent the church's mission and vision.
